The Charles Harpur Critical Archive is a digital archive and a scholarly edition of the poetic works of Charles Harpur (1813xE2x80x9368), the most important poet of colonial New South Wales. A Romantic celebrator of Australian landscape, a political satirist, a love poet and early environmentalist, Harpur and his works remain relevant today. But they are not easily accessible. This is because Harpur was a prolific poet and an inveterate reviser. Extant today in manuscript, and in colonial newspaper and other printed forms, are over 2,700 versions of his 686 works in verse, as well as the often fascinating prose notes that he wrote for them. Most of these versions have never been published xE2x80x93 until now.
